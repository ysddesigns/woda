Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Woda ("the app") is an unofficial companion app for the 2026 World Cup, showing fixtures, standings, teams, and venues. This page explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Data we collect

Woda does not require an account and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us. There are no analytics or tracking SDKs in the app.

Location

If you tap "Show my distance" on a venue screen, the app asks for one-time access to your device's location so it can calculate your distance from that stadium. This is entirely optional, the location is used only on your device for that calculation, and it is never sent to us or stored.

Notifications

If you enable match notifications in Settings, the app requests permission to send local push notifications about matches. You can disable this at any time from the app's Settings screen or your device's system settings.

Match data source

Fixtures, scores, standings, and venue information are pulled from a public third-party data API. Woda is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by FIFA or any official World Cup organizer.

Third parties

Woda does not sell or share any data with third parties, because it does not collect any personal data in the first place.

Children's privacy

Woda does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
